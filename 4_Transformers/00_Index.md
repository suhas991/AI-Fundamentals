## Key Concepts

- **Self-Attention**: The mechanism that allows tokens to attend to each other
- **Multi-Head Attention**: Parallel attention operations learning different patterns
- **Encoder-Decoder**: The original architecture for sequence-to-sequence tasks
- **Positional Encoding**: How Transformers understand token order
- **Scaling Laws**: How performance improves with size and compute

## Why This Matters

Transformers are the foundation of modern LLMs like GPT, BERT, Claude, and more. Understanding this architecture is essential for:

- Working with pre-trained models
- Fine-tuning for specific tasks
- Building AI applications
- Interview preparation
- Research and development
