## Quick Definitions

- **AI**: The broad field of building systems that perform tasks requiring intelligence.
- **ML**: A subset of AI where systems learn patterns from data.
- **DL**: A subset of ML based on multi-layer neural networks.
- **GenAI**: A subset of DL focused on generating new content (text, images, audio, video, code).

## Recommended Study Order

1. Read AI foundations and categories.
2. Move to ML paradigms (supervised, unsupervised, reinforcement).
3. Learn deep neural architectures (CNNs, RNNs, Transformers).
4. Study generative systems and practical deployment patterns.
